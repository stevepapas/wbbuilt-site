About WB Built

W B B U I L T is committed to creating spaces that inspire. We are dedicated to ensuring that our projects are not only visually stunning but also accessible to people with disabilities, providing equal ease and enjoyment for all visitors.

Accessibility

At W B B U I L T we believe that web accessibility is fundamental. Our goal is to provide a seamless browsing experience for visitors with disabilities, achieved through adherence to the latest WCAG guidelines and achieving a high level of accessibility. We have implemented various measures to facilitate accessibility, including:

Features

Our dedication to accessibility is reflected in the following features:

  • Prioritizing clear and concise content structure
  • Providing alternative text for all images
  • Ensuring compatibility with assistive technologies such as screen readers and keyboard navigation
  • Adhering to optimal color contrast and readability
  • Minimizing the use of motion and ensuring all multimedia content is accessible

Our Physical Accessibility Arrangements

WB Built acknowledges that certain pages may have partial compliance due to third-party content, and we are committed to addressing these issues for a fully accessible experience.

Physical Accessibility

In addition to our digital efforts, WB Built ensures physical accessibility in all our office locations and project sites. Our facilities are equipped with a range of accessibility arrangements, including but not limited to accessible parking, ramps, audio induction loops, and elevators, to ensure a seamless experience for all visitors.
